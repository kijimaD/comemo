    cmd/gc: optimize existence-only map lookups
    
    The compiler converts 'val, ok = m[key]' to
    
            tmp, ok = <runtime call>
            val = *tmp
    
    For lookups of the form '_, ok = m[key]',
    the second statement is unnecessary.
    By not generating it we save a nil check.
