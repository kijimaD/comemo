    runtime: gdb support: use parse_and_eval to get the goroutine id
    
    This enables to loop over some goroutines, e.g. to print the
    backtrace of goroutines 1 to 9:
    
            set $i = 1
            while $i < 10
            printf "backtrace of goroutine %d:\n", $i
            goroutine $i++ bt
            end
