    runtime: drop CLONE_PARENT
    
    The functionality we want (shared ppid) is implied
    by CLONE_THREAD already, and CLONE_PARENT
    causes problems if the Go program is pid 1 (init).
    
    See issue 1406 for more details.
    
    Fixes #1406.

    CC=golang-dev
