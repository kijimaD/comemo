    html: parse misnested formatting tags according to the HTML5 spec.
    This is the "adoption agency" algorithm.
    
    The test case input is "<a><p>X<a>Y</a>Z</p></a>". The correct parse is:
    | <html>
    |   <head>
    |   <body>
    |     <a>
    |     <p>
    |       <a>
    |         "X"
    |       <a>
    |         "Y"
    |       "Z"
