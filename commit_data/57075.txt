    cmd/asm: fix encoding errors for FMOVD and FMOVS instructions on arm64
    
    The encoding of instructions "FMOVD F1, ZR" and "FMOVS F1, ZR" is wrong,
    the assembler encodes them as "FMOVD F1, F31" and "FMOVS F1, F31". This
    CL fixes the bug.
