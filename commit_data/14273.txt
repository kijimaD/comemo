    cmd/gc: avoid %#x of 0
    
    Plan 9 and Go's lib9/fmt disagree on whether %#x includes the 0x prefix
    when printing 0, because ANSI C gave bad advice long ago.
    
    Avoiding that case makes binaries compiled on different systems compatible.
