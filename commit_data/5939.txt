    crypto/x509: unwrap Subject Key Identifier
    
    RFC 5280, 4.2.1.2 says:
      SubjectKeyIdentifier ::= KeyIdentifier
      KeyIdentifier ::= OCTET STRING
    
    Previously, we were failing to unwrap the second level of OCTET STRING
    encoding.
    
    Fixes #993.
