    cmd/link: recompute heapPos after copyHeap
    
    Immediately after a forward Seek, the offset we're writing to is
    beyond len(buf)+len(heap):
    
    |<--- buf --->|<--- heap --->|
                                        ^
                                        off
    
    If we do a copyHeap at this point, the new heapPos should not be
    0:
    
    |<---------- buf ----------->|<-heap->|
                                        ^
                                        off
    
    Recompute it.
    
    For #42082.
