    cmd/internal/obj/loong64: add support for instructions FCOPYSIGN.{S/D} and FCLASS.{S/D}
    
    Go asm syntax:
            FCOPYSG{F/D}    FK, FJ, FD
            FCLASSF{F/D}    FJ, FD
    
    Equivalent platform assembler syntax:
            fcopysign.{s/d} fd, fj, fk
            fclass.{s/d}    fd, fj
