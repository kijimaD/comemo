    cmd/asm: add asimd instruction 'rev16' on arm64
    
    Add support to the asimd instruction rev16 which reverses elements in
    16-bit halfwords.
    
    syntax:
            VREV16 <Vn>.<T>, <Vd>.<T>
    <T> should be either B8 or B16.
