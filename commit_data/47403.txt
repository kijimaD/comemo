    cmd/asm: add rotr/drotr for mips64
    
    This CL encodes:
    
    ROTR rd, rt, sa
    ROTRV rd, rt, rs

    DROTR rd, rt, sa
    DROTR32 rd, rt, sa
    DROTRV rd, rt, rs
    
    => ROTRV (SCON|REG), (REG,)? REG
    
    Note: ROTRV will handle const over 32
