    io/ioutil: support predictable prefix,suffix for TempDir with *
    
    Allow TempDir to create directories with predictable
    prefixes and suffixes, separated by the last "*", for example:
        "prefix*suffix"
    will now expand to
        "prefix" + <RANDOM_VALUE> + "suffix"
