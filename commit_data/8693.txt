    fmt: debugging formats for characters: %+q %#U
    %+q uses strconv.Quote[Rune]ToASCII, guaranteeing ASCII-only output.
    %#U a quoted character if the rune is printable: 'x'=U+0078; otherwise
    it's as before: U+000A.
